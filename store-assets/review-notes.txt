SIMULATED PROFILES (BOT ACCOUNTS)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

To ensure that new users have a positive first experience and see real matches right away, we have pre-seeded the platform with 26 simulated profiles (15 female, 10 male, 1 non-binary). These profiles:

- Have fully completed questionnaires and realistic bio descriptions
- Use stock photos licensed for app use
- Do NOT send messages or initiate contact
- Are clearly part of our moderated seed dataset, not real users impersonating individuals
- Will be progressively replaced by real users as the platform grows

This practice is common and necessary for early-stage dating apps to prevent the "empty app" problem that discourages genuine user sign-ups. All simulated profiles are disclosed in our Terms of Service.

ADMOB REWARDED ADS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Rewarded video ads (via Google AdMob) are shown ONLY when a free-tier user has exhausted their 10 daily likes. A prompt appears offering the user the option to watch a short video ad in exchange for 5 additional likes.

- Ads are never shown unsolicited or interstitially
- The user must actively tap "Watch a video" to trigger the ad
- Gold subscribers never see any ads
- During review, AdMob may serve test ads or no ads depending on the build environment; this does not affect app functionality
